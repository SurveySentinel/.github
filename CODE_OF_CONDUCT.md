# Code of Conduct

SurveySentinel is committed to maintaining a respectful and professional community.

We expect all participants to:

- be respectful
- be constructive
- remain professional
- focus on technical discussion
- avoid harassment or abusive behaviour
- respect intellectual property

---

## Unacceptable Behaviour

Examples include:

- harassment
- discrimination
- abusive language
- trolling
- spam
- personal attacks
- publishing confidential information

---

## Enforcement

The SurveySentinel maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, lock discussions or restrict repository access where appropriate.

---

Thank you for helping maintain a welcoming community.
